Future Work

Future development will expand the database to include a wider range of products and material types.

We plan to optimize SafeSwap for embedded platforms like Raspberry Pi to enable field applications at grocery markets, restaurants, and waste sites.

Integration with cloud-based data aggregation will enable large-scale mapping of microplastic contamination patterns and support policymaking efforts.

Additional improvements will focus on better material classification, object segmentation, and real-time feedback for an enhanced user experience.
